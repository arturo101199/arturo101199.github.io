Garden Battle

The Project

This project was my Final Degree Work. The game is a battle simulator, where 4 different types of insects fight each other in order to win the game. Each one has its own strategy in order to kill the rest. The player can choose how many animals want of each type...

The length of the project was 4 months. We released a fully playable demo and decided to move on to other projects.
